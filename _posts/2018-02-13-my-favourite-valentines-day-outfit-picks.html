In the run-up to Valentines Day, I've pulled together my top outfit picks for whether you're out celebrating Valentines, Galentines, or just having a night in.

We all like to dress to impress, and obviously, we all need to know where we’re going so that we can dress accordingly. The first top pick from my outfit choice is this cute t-shirt dress from Boohoo. This dress is perfect for a ‘cheeky Nando’s’ or for a pizza feast, the loose material allows room for a food baby – but you also look super-hot and can team this with knee high boots or a pair of high heels to dress it up even more. This look is great for an in-between restaurant when money is tight, but romance and food are still on the cards!

For those who love to be wined and dined at fancy restaurants, this blazer dress is one of my top picks for the occasion. It’s classy, sexy, and shows just about the right amount of skin. This choice goes against the stereotypical ‘red dress' and will really make you stand out from the crowd. Just make sure the red wine and spaghetti sauce is kept at arm’s length in this little white number. From £40 at Missguided, this would definitely be a must on my shopping list.

It’s accessories time! And who doesn’t love a sultry nude? My tassel earing obsession hasn’t faded, and I’m still on the hunt to own a collection. These gorgeous nude tassel earrings for £8 from Pretty Little Thing scream classy and will also draw his eyes up to your face. Ah-hem… These earrings are great to team with an up-do and dramatic make-up to really make a statement.

These gorgeous studded shoes would go perfectly with the tassel earrings and most outfits. They also have a ‘Valentino’ feel about them. This style of shoe is my absolute favourite at the minute – and I need another pair to add to the ones I already have!

And finally, if you're like me - you're not that bothered about going out to eat for Valentines, and would rather spend the night at home in PJ's with some home cooked steaks then check out this cute set. These PJ's from Boohoo are absolutely perfect for a Galentines night - because who doesn't love fries more than guys?

Whatever you’re doing this Valentines, I hope you enjoy my top outfit picks – and absolutely boss this Valentines/Galentines!

Sophie Amelia
