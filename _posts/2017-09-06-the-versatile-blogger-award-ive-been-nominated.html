I am so happy to announce that I have been nominated for the Versatile Blogger Award, many thanks to Kimberly from Life of Kimberly. I feel so privileged and proud of my little blog to of received this, especially after just receiving the Liebster Award also!

Whilst these nominations/awards may not seem like a lot to some people, with my blog having an age of approximately 2 months - I'm so pleased to see that people are reading and enjoying my posts! This award in particular gave me a great sense of achievement as I do try to keep my content as varied and interesting as possible for you lovely lot to read. Your encouragement reminds me why I started Sophie Amelia in the first place!

The Rules

  • Thank the person who nominated you
  • Share 7 facts about yourself
  • Display the Versatile Blogger award logo in the post
  • Nominate up to 15 other bloggers of your choice
  • Link your nominees and let them know of your nomination
