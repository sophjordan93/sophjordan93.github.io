 I’m super excited to announce my first even collaboration post. Kate and I both have an interest in travel, so we thought it would be a great idea to come together and write a post. Here’s an introduction piece that Kate wrote about herself and her blog:

girlmasked.wordpress.com is lifestyle and mental health blog. It has the occasional travel post and book review. I am hoping to branch into beauty product reviews sometime soon.

I have had this blog for a little over a year now and I am really enjoying improving my blog, posting and getting to know other bloggers.

Aside from blogging in spare time I like to read and write poems. I also enjoy spending time with my family and friends and learning new things. I love to bake and go on long walks in forests/beaches.

I really enjoyed doing this collab post with Sophie, it’s been fun and I hope to work with her again sometime.

 

How many countries have you visited?

Me: I’m not sure off the top of my head, but from what I can remember approximately 15? This number needs making bigger!

Kate: Just two! Hungary and Italy!

 

Which was your favourite country?

Me: It’s got to be Thailand, there was just something about it I can’t put my finger on. The food, the culture, the scenery – I spent nearly 2 months there, and I’m itching to go back!

Kate: Definitely Italy! We stayed in Lake Garda and then travelled to places like Verona and Venice. I loved it. There was something so magical about it! The scenery was amazing, so many different streets, mountains and flowers!

 

What 3 places are next on your bucket list to visit?

Me: Santorini, Italy and more parts of the USA.

Kate: London, Paris and Seville

 

What is your go-to travel necessity?

Me: It’s got to be a camera of some sort. Whether it’s my iPhone, Canon, or an underwater camera. Snapping photos on holiday is a must!

Kate: My camera too!! I take a crazy amount of photographs on holidays! Probably a book and notebook too with a pen to keep me occupied on long journeys!

 

Where are you going on your next travels?

Me: I’m heading off for a weekend in Amsterdam in November for my birthday, it’s somewhere I’ve always wanted to go!

Kate: I have no idea, I’ve no plans!! I’d love to go to London next and travel around the UK.

 

 What is your go to summer holiday outfit?

Me: I need to find a summery, floaty dress for every holiday. Without fail.

Kate: Simple shorts and t- shirt with my favourite runners and a bracelet!

 

What’s the best thing you’ve ever bought in another country?

Me: This is so hard, as I love to bring stuff back with me. It’s got to be between my beloved red furry gilet from NYC, my hand-made customised jewelled ring from India, and my underwater phone pouch I bought in Mexico.

Kate: A small mask based on the Comedia Del Arte Style masks which I bought in Italy last year and it is hanging on my wall.

I hope you enjoyed our collaboration post. I've thoroughly enjoyed doing this and would love to work with more bloggers! Get in touch :)
